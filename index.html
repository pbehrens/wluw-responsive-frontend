upcoming shows

Thursday @ 6pm
Radio One Chicago with Hex, Jeannie, and Sandra

We're Radio One Chicago, broadcasting every Thursday night from 6:00 - 8:00 p.m. Hosted by Hex, Jeannie and Sandra, the show focuses on all things Chicago especially the great music the city produces. Chicago bands and artists, send us your music and projects. Email us at RadioOneChicago@gmail.com & check out our blog at www.RadioOneChicago.com Twitter: @radioonechicago

Thursday @ 10pm
Abstract Science Crew with Chris Widman, Luke Stokes, and Henry Self

A weekly exploration of electronic music and its roots with hosts Chris Widman, Luke Stokes and Henry Self featuring guest DJ sets & live performances. abstract science is a highly entertaining and educational exploration of what we like to call "future music" - the constant evolution of modern electronic music in its varied forms on a global level, from roots in funk, jazz, dub and soul. In a time when the majority of electronic music is becoming mass-produced and highly disposable, we engage and excite our audience with genuinely innovative and relevant programming. abstract science is mandatory listening for those who realize that popular culture is neither. for more info: www.abstractscience.net www.myspace.com/abstractscience www.facebook.com/pages/abstract-science/86948944187?ref=s

Friday @ 2am
TurnTableTerrorist with Terry C.

Each week theTurnTableTerrorist (aka Terry C.) takes you on a bass-heavy sonic journey through the world of dub, starting with old-school roots and covering dub and dub influenced music including new-school roots, ambient dub, dubhop, dubstep, digi-dub, Asian dub and more from around the globe. Rivers of bass flowing into oceans of dub...all from the shores of Lake Michigan. If you miss a show, you can listen any time from the archives. Just search for "echo beach" at www.archive.org or at www.talawa.fr

Friday @ 12pm
Diggy's Funk-Soul Explosion with Anthony D.

A blend of old school R&B, electronic jazz and hip/hop, with a hint of classic rock from the 70's, 80's, and 90's.
