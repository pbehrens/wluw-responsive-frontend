Our Curriculum

Our curriculum will prepare our students to become the most competent, most creative, and most innovative people in their chosen fields within and beyond the Global Sports Economy.

We are designing our curriculum around by three basic principles:

  • We believe that knowledge is relative
  • We believe that experience is the best teacher
  • We believe that interdisciplinary experiences cultivate creativity.

Our curriculum will prepare our students to become the most competent, most creative, and most innovative people in their chosen fields within and beyond the Global Sports Economy.

Our curriculum will emphasize:

  • Interdisciplinary education in the areas of sports, music, and society
  • Emotional intelligence, critical thinking, and communication skills
  • Experiential learning in the areas of sports science, sport technology, and sports media
  • Global awareness and cultural learning through the international boarding school

Ultimately, we aim on reaching kids early: take a spark, fan into flame, one day becomes a blaze.
